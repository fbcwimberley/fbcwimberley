## Design Context

### Users
Parents and guardians at First Baptist Church Wimberley who are trying to understand the faith-formation pathway for their children and students. They may be scanning quickly on a phone, preparing for a milestone training, or looking for confidence about what comes next.

### Brand Personality
Bold, warm, formative, trustworthy. The page should feel spiritually grounded and professionally led without becoming corporate or generic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a bold animated formation-roadmap direction: immersive, polished, spacious, and memorable. Motion should guide attention through the pathway and make the sequence feel intentional. Keep the existing church brand fonts and tokens for consistency, but use stronger hierarchy, rhythm, and interaction detail.

### Design Principles
- Lead with clarity for parents: what the pathway is, who each milestone serves, and what action or training matters.
- Make the seven milestones feel like a guided journey, not a static list of repeated cards.
- Use motion to reveal structure and progression, with reduced-motion support.
- Preserve warmth and trust through readable copy, generous spacing, and church-brand colors.
- Avoid generic design tells: no gradient text, no thick side-stripe card borders, no nested cards.
